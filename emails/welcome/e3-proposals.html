Winning the work

You think the call decided it. It didn't.

Great call, they laughed at your joke, you hung up sure you had it. Then the proposal goes out and nothing.

Three ways proposals lose. Which one's yours?

You opened with your bio They're scanning for "did she get my problem." Make them dig and they stop.
You quoted before you knew the scope Underprice it and resent it by week three. Overprice it and lose to whoever asked one more question.
You ended with "let me know!" You handed the next move to someone who forgets by lunch.

Fix the last one first. Swap "let me know" for a date:

"If this works, I'll send the agreement Thursday and we start Monday."
Everybody skips that line. It's the one that turns a maybe into a start date.
